Why Recruit at St.Joseph's?

St. Joseph's College Of Engineering is situated in the midst of most of the leading Companies at the Old Mahabalipuram Road in Chennai. It was started in the year 1994 with the notion of catering to the needs of manpower in technology for the country. With the students and staff drawn from most of the states of our country, the students were exposed to various social, economic and cultural lives. Ever since its established it has its speedy growth in Co-curricular, Extracurricular and Academic activities. It offers ten Under Graduate Courses and Post Graduate Courses in Engineering and Technology. As far as teaching Faculty is concerned our College has Profound Professors with the qualification of PhD and Post Doctorates. Ever since its established.
