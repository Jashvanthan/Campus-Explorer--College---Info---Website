Why St.Joseph's?

The uniqueness of Centre for Training and Placement of this prestigious Institution is prevalent through the execution of meticulous planning and systematic schedule. It has been functioning untiringly towards the benefits of the students to strike out a difference in the corporate world. Our college provides a friendly and challenging environment in order to meet the demands of today's competitive world. As the candle spreads, and the mirror reflects the light, the department spreads the light to reflect the dreams and goals of the students. Instead of waiting for opportunities, opportunities knock at your door. The infrastructure too enhances the facilities of the Training and Placement Programmes.

Objectives

  • It nurtures Industry institute interaction and mould and mentors the students to be fit for the placement.
  • It also provides job opportunities by bringing companies for campus recruitment to all the eligible final year students.
  • It enhances and employs current practical and technical knowledge of the students. In addition, it makes the students plug and play by imparting soft skills and employability skills.
  • It networks with International Industries and Professional Societies through MoUs and maintains worldwide relations to handle the issues related to campus recruitment, etc.

Why Recruit at St.Joseph's?

St. Joseph's College Of Engineering is situated in the midst of most of the leading Companies at the Old Mahabalipuram Road in Chennai. It was started in the year 1994 with the notion of catering to the needs of manpower in technology for the country. With the students and staff drawn from most of the states of our country, the students were exposed to various social, economic and cultural lives. Ever since its establishment it has had speedy growth in Co-curricular, Extracurricular and Academic activities. It offers ten Under Graduate Courses and Post Graduate Courses in Engineering and Technology. As far as teaching Faculty is concerned our College has Profound Professors with the qualification of PhD and Post Doctorates.
