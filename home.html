St.joseph's college of engineering

St. Joseph's Group of Institutions, a beacon of engineering excellence for over three decades, prioritizes innovation and academic rigor. Through cutting-edge technologies, expert guidance, and holistic development programs, it prepares students to excel in a competitive global environment, fostering growth, resilience, and community spirit.

Dr. B. Babu Manoharan, the esteemed Chairman of St. Joseph's College of Engineering, is a visionary leader who has dedicated his career to shaping the future of countless students. With a philosophy of "simple living and high thinking," he has played a pivotal role in fostering an environment of academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. His extensive experience as a Founder Director of Sathyabama Engineering College and Director of St. Joseph's College of Engineering reflects his unwavering commitment to nurturing technically skilled, intellectually enlightened, and morally grounded individuals prepared to face the challenges of a dynamic world.
