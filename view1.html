SJCE Chennai Admission Process 2025

The steps to apply for SJCE Chennai for the year 2025 are mentioned below:

Step 1:Entrance Exam: Appear for the TNEA and TANCET exam and obtain a valid score.
Step 2: Application: Apply online through the official SJCE Chennai website.
Step 3: Counselling: Participate in the TNEA or TANCET counselling process, where seat allotment is based on exam score, preferences, and availability.
Step 4: Document Verification: Verify original documents at the designated help centers.
Step 5: Fee Payment: Pay the admission fee to confirm the seat.
